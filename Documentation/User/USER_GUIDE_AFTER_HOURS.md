# User Guide: After Hours Notice

This feature helps manage user expectations by displaying a message when they attempt to create a ticket outside of your business hours.

## Configuration

Go to **StackBoost > After Hours Notice**.

## Settings

*   **Enable Feature:** Toggle the notice on or off.
*   **Business Hours:**
    *   **After Hours Start:** The hour (24-hour format) when your day ends (e.g., `17` for 5:00 PM).
    *   **Before Hours End:** The hour (24-hour format) when your day begins (e.g., `8` for 8:00 AM).
*   **Weekends:** Check "Include All Weekends" to show the notice all day on Saturday and Sunday.
*   **Holidays:** Enter specific dates (MM-DD-YYYY) where the notice should appear all day.
    *   *Example:* `12-25-2024`
*   **Message:** Use the text editor to customize the message shown to users.
    *   *Example:* "Our office is currently closed. We will respond to your request on the next business day."

## Usage
Once enabled, the message will automatically appear at the top of the SupportCandy "Create Ticket" form during the configured times.
